Frequently Asked Questions

How does the local AI model work?

Omodo includes a built-in LLM manager that lets you download and manage AI models directly within the app — no extra tools required. You can also use Ollama, a free open-source tool for running local models, if you prefer. Either way, all AI processing happens entirely on your Mac.

macOS says the app is damaged or can't be opened?

This is expected during the beta — Omodo is not yet signed or notarized with Apple. When you first open the app, macOS may show a warning or suggest moving it to the trash. To allow it: open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and you'll see a message about Omodo being blocked. Click "Open Anyway" to allow it. You only need to do this once.

Is my data really private?

Yes — 100%. Omodo processes everything on your Mac using a local AI model (managed through the built-in LLM manager or Ollama). There are no cloud servers, no API calls to external services, no accounts, no analytics, and no telemetry. Your emails, files, and notes never leave your machine.

What Mac do I need?

Omodo requires a Mac with Apple Silicon (M1 or later) running macOS 14 (Sonoma) or newer. Apple Silicon is needed for efficient local LLM inference.

Which email providers are supported?

Omodo works through Apple Mail, so any email account you've added to Mail — Gmail, Outlook, iCloud, Yahoo, or any IMAP provider — is supported. You choose which mailboxes to monitor.

Is Omodo free?

Omodo is completely free during the beta period. We'll share more about pricing as we approach a stable release.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Since Omodo runs AI models locally on your Mac, you don't need an internet connection for AI processing. You'll only need internet for receiving new emails or syncing calendar events through Apple's apps.

What LLM models are supported?

Omodo works with popular models including Llama, Mistral, Gemma, Phi, and more. You can download models directly through Omodo's built-in LLM manager or use Ollama. Choose your preferred model in Settings based on your performance and quality preferences.

How do I get started?

Download Omodo and launch the app — the onboarding wizard will guide you through permissions, downloading a local AI model (built-in or via Ollama), and choosing which sources to monitor. You'll be up and running in minutes.
