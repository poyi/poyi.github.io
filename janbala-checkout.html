Project Description

I worked on the redesign of the checkout flow to introduce a wizard like experience (from single page) to reduce the page clutterness and to accommodate for the addition of new product upsell (insurance).

There are three checkout steps. Step 1: Review selected flights/hotel. Step 2: Fill out traveler details. Step 3: Review add-on and submit payment to book.

The confirmation page summarizes the purchase and displays call to actions to sign up (if not signed up/in before the flow) and call to action that leads to the hotel flow.
